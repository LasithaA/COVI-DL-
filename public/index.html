Covid Detection App

What is [name of app]

[name of app] is for users to find out their chances of surviving if they get Covid-19 (with % accuracy) based on their current pre-existing medical conditions. They can also find out whether or not they have Covid-19 (with % accuracy) based on their chest-xray.


The app uses an image of the patient's X-Ray to classify the user into one of 3 categories: Covid-19, Pneumonia, or Healthy the image. Trained and deployed using Google Cloud AutoML with 94% precision. The Survival Chance part of the app uses the patient's medical information to predict how likely they are to recover from Covid-19. Trained using a regression neural network model with 81% precision.



** Model is created from data gathered from Mexico and is reflective of their population's circumstances

How does it Work?

(Recommended for Everyone)

Find out your chances of surviving Covid-19 by entering in your medical information

(Recommended for Health Professionals)

Determine if patient has SARS-CoV-2 by uploading image of their chest x-ray
